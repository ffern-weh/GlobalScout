Food

Indulge in Japan's world-renowned cuisine. Experience sushi, ramen, tempura, and okonomiyaki, each offering a unique taste of Japanese culinary tradition. Don't miss out on regional specialties like Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki or Kyoto's kaiseki dining.

Places to Visit

Tokyo
The bustling capital city known for its skyscrapers and vibrant culture.
Kyoto
Famous for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses.
Hokkaido
Known for its volcanoes, natural hot springs (onsen), and ski areas.
Nara
Famous for its park, which is home to hundreds of freely roaming deer, and its significant temples.
Okinawa
Known for its tropical climate, beautiful beaches, and coral reefs.
Hiroshima
Known for its Peace Memorial Park, which commemorates the 1945 atomic bombing.

Hotels

Find the best places to stay during your trip to Japan. Choose from luxurious hotels in Tokyo, traditional ryokans in Kyoto, or beach resorts in Okinawa. Many hotels offer unique experiences such as onsen (hot spring) baths and kaiseki (multi-course) dinners. For a truly authentic experience, consider staying in a temple lodging (shukubo) where you can participate in meditation and enjoy vegetarian Buddhist cuisine.
