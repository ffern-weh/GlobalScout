Must try food

Tteokbokki
Tteokbokki is renowned for its addictive combination of chewy rice cakes and a bold, sweet-spicy sauce.
kimbap
Kimbap is celebrated for its flavorful, bite-sized rolls of rice, vegetables, and protein wrapped in seaweed.
Jajangmyeon
Jajangmyeon is beloved for its savory black bean sauce served over chewy noodles.
Fried Chicken
Korean fried chicken is famous for its ultra-crispy skin and juicy meat, often coated in a sweet and spicy sauce.
Bibimbap
Bibimbap is known for its vibrant mix of rice, vegetables, and meat, topped with a spicy gochujang sauce.
Doenjang Jjigae
Doenjang JjigaeDoenjang Jjigae is prized for its rich, hearty broth made from fermented soybean paste.
Japchae
Japchae is cherished for its savory-sweet stir-fried glass noodles mixed with a vibrant medley of vegetables and meat.
