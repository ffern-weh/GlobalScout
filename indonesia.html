Must try food

Nasi Goreng
Indonesia's iconic fried rice, bursting with flavor and tradition.
Batagor
Crispy fried dumplings with a savory peanut sauce.
Ketoprak
A delightful mix of rice noodles, tofu, and vegetables in a savory peanut sauce.
Ketupat Sayur
Rice cakes in rich coconut milk curry, a festive favorite.
Mie Ayam
Flavorful chicken noodles, a street food classic.
Satay
Grilled skewers of marinated meat, served with a delicious peanut sauce.
Soto
Aromatic Indonesian soup with tender meat, vegetables, and a rich, flavorful broth.

Hotels

Light Blue Villa
Featuring a restaurant, the resort is close to several noted attractions, around a 10-minute walk from Ngandong Beach.
Holiday Inn Resort Bali
An IHG Hotel provides accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness center and a shared lounge.
Orchardz Jayakarta
15-minute drive from Ancol featuring modern furnished rooms with private bathrooms.
Adhidrawa Ubud
It offers accommodations with access to a hot tub, with pool views, this accommodation features a terrace and a swimming pool.
Salili Bungalow
Offering beachfront accommodations in Gili Air, Salili Bungalow is a convenient 10-minute walk to Gili Air Harbor.
Vanilla Garden
Offering garden views, Vanilla Garden in Tetebatu features accommodations, a garden, and a bar.

Must do things in Indonesia

Sacred Monkey Sanctuary
The Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary is a nature reserve and temple complex in Ubud, Bali, home to over 1260 long-tailed macaques.
Ceking Rice Terrace
A UNESCO World Heritage site, the area is trendy during sunrise and sunset over cascading rice fields.
Campuhan Ridge Walk
This is a short hike up with a beautiful view of the sunset.
Tanah Lot Temple
Tanah Lot Temple is one of Bali's most important landmarks, famed for its unique offshore setting and sunset backdrops.
Ijen Crater
Embark on an adventurous journey to Ijen Crater, a quiet yet active volcano.
Waterbom Bali
Exciting water slides through tropical parks providing hours of fun and entertainment for the young and young at heart!
